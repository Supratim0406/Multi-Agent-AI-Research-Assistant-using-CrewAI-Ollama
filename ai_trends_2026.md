## Conclusions & Recommendations
- The integration of AI into everyday life is expected to increase significantly in the coming years.
- Companies should be aware of the potential risks associated with AI-driven decision automation and take measures to mitigate them.
- Energy demand will likely pose a major challenge for AI deployment, and efforts should be made to address this issue.
- The global market for AI is projected to grow significantly, creating opportunities for job creation.
- Companies are encouraged to adopt an enterprise-wide strategy centered on a top-down program to stay competitive in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.
