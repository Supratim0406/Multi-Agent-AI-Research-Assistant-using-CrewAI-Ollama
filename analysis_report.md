## Key Insights
- AI will become more integrated into everyday life with an increase in Silicon Valley apps using Chinese open models (Anabelle Nicoud, IBM)
- The global AI market is projected to grow significantly from $190.6 billion in 2020 to $602.36 billion by 2026 (MarketsandMarkets)
- There is a concern about the potential deflation of the AI bubble and its impact on the economy (Erik Brynjolfsson, MIT Sloan School of Management)
- Energy demand will be a major bottleneck for AI deployment (AIBusiness)
- AI-driven decision automation poses a risk of catastrophic loss (Gartner)
- More companies are expected to adopt an enterprise-wide strategy centered on a top-down program (PwC)
- There will be a notable increase in news mentions of "AI" and "suicide" in media databases by 2026 compared to 2025 (Michael Cook, Understanding AI)
